The OECD Due Diligence Guidance prescribes a 4-step framework for responsible sourcing from conflict-affected areas. Step 1 requires companies to identify and assess risks in the supply chain. Step 2 establishes a strong company management system including a supply chain policy. Step 3 requires companies to report annually to the OECD Secretariat. Step 4 involves carrying out independent third-party audits of smelters and refiners.
